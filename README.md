# GameZone_Store_Analysis (2019-2021)
GamzeZone, a company founded in 2018, sells new and refurbished gaming products worldwide across multiple channels. This project aims to provide insights and deliver recommendations to the Product, Sales, Marketing, and Loyalty Management teams.

Project Objectives:<br>
- Product Performance Analysis: To assess how different products perform in terms of net sales, refund rate, and brand performance in general and regional markets.


Product Performance:
-- 
1. Overall Performance:
      
    - GameZone company has achieved Total Net Sales 4.92M, Order Quantity **21718**, with an Net Average Order Value of **$227**. 
    
    - The top-net-selling product is a **27-inch 4K gaming monitor**, accounting for **$1.5M** and **31% of total net sales**.

    - Highest order quantity is presented by Nintendo Switch with **10288**. 
    
    - The **Consoles** category has the highest net sales (**$2.6M**, **53% of total**) and order quantity (**11241**).

1. North America (NA) Dominance: The Teal line representing NA consistently remains the highest-performing region, peaking at over $450K in Net Sales during Q2 2020. 
  
2. The "Peak": There was a massive acceleration in sales across all regions starting in January 2020, and sales fell at the end of 2020.
  
3. Consoles is the best-selling (over 10K) and highest total net sales ($2.6M) category according to the customers, with around twice the sales of the second category, Monitors ($1.53M Net Sales with 4.6K Orders).
